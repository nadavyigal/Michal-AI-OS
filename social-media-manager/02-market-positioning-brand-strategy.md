# Step 2 — Market Positioning and Brand Strategy

## Brand
**SHINE by Michal Slonim.**

## What she represents
Change generated from the inside, not from controlling the outside. Her own words: "מבפנים
החוצה" (from the inside out), "לא צריך לחכות שהצד השני ישתנה — השינוי מתחיל בך" (you don't
have to wait for the other person to change — change starts with you).

## The unique angle that sets her apart

**vs. couples-coaching competitors:** that space splits into two camps — purely spiritual/
mystical voice (generic "your soul chose this person") or purely tactical scripts
(communication "hacks" with no emotional depth). Michal sits in neither. She combines deep
emotional/somatic work (Satir method, childhood-wound healing) with field-tested practicality
from 12 years actually managing people. That specific combination — lived managerial
credibility *plus* emotional depth — is rare in this niche and hard to copy.

**vs. leadership-coaching competitors:** that space is usually frameworks-only (time
management, KPIs, communication templates) without addressing the manager's internal state.
Michal's "stability before strategy" angle — you can't lead calmly from a state you don't
have — is differentiated there too, and nobody else can credibly claim both sides of this at
once.

## Brand voice & tone
Pulled directly from the Voice doc (§2): direct, warm, has lived what she teaches, leads with
the felt problem before the framework. Never clinical/jargon-heavy, never hype-y
growth-marketing voice, never generic self-help platitudes.
