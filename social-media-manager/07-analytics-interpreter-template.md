# Step 7 — Analytics Interpreter and Optimizer

**This step has not actually run.** There is no real Instagram/Facebook Insights data yet —
this file is the framework to use the moment she shares one, not a finished analysis. Don't
present this as more than a template tonight.

## What to ask her for (or pull together, once access exists)

- Reach and engagement rate per post, last 90 days
- Follower growth curve
- Top 5 posts by **saves and shares** specifically — not likes; saves/shares are the real
  "this helped me" signal
- Profile visits → website/link-in-bio clicks
- Story completion rate
- The posting times she's actually used historically, with their reach

## How to interpret it once the data exists

- **What's working:** map the top-saved/shared posts back to the 5 pillars in Step 3. The
  pillar that dominates the top posts is the one to lean into harder.
- **What's not working:** low story-completion or low-save feed posts usually mean
  info-only content without an emotional hook — cross-check against the Step 5 hook bank.
- **Content gaps:** compare her actual posted mix against the target 35/20/20/15/10
  distribution from Step 3. Flag drift in either direction.
- **Optimal frequency:** validate the 3–4×/week hypothesis from Step 4 against actual
  reach-per-post trend. For a solo creator, more volume isn't automatically better — check
  whether reach-per-post degrades as frequency increases before recommending more.
- **90-day roadmap:** propose 3 concrete KPIs (e.g. average saves/post, DM conversations
  started per week, course-link clicks) with a bi-weekly review checkpoint — not a single
  end-of-quarter check-in.
