# Step 4 — 30-Day Content Calendar (Instagram, cross-posted to Facebook)

## A deliberate choice on cadence

Her own content-strategy note says 3–4 feed posts a week — that's the real cadence used here,
**not** the generic "post daily" advice most templates default to. Daily posting isn't
realistic for a solo coach already complaining about outsourced-copy flatness, and pushing
volume she can't sustain would recreate the same problem with AI instead of a copywriter.
14 feed posts across 30 days, plus a lighter daily story habit (below).

**Posting times are a hypothesis, not data** — no real Instagram Insights exist yet (see Step
7). Default: evenings 20:00–22:00 and weekend mornings 9:00–11:00, based on the audience
profile in Step 1. Validate and correct once real data exists.

## Stories (lighter, daily habit — separate from feed production)

5–10 minutes/day: a poll or question sticker, a behind-the-scenes moment, or a repost of a
feed highlight with one added line. This is the engagement layer, not the content-production
layer — keep it light enough that it actually happens every day.

## Handoff to the Gem

This table gives concept, format, hook, and CTA — not finished copy. For each scheduled day,
paste the hook + pillar into the **Content/Voice Gem** (`gems/content-voice-gem.md`) to get
the final, voice-matched, ready-to-publish post body. The calendar tells you *what and when*;
the Gem tells you *exactly how to say it*.
