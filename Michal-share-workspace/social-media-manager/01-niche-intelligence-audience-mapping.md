# Step 1 — Niche Intelligence and Audience Mapping

## The niche is dual — this matters for everything downstream

- **Primary, current public presence:** personal/couples relationship coaching, Satir method (her existing Instagram identity, `michal_slonim_life_coach`).
- **Secondary, real but not yet public:** organizational/management coaching, drawing on 12 years running an insurance agency (~20 employees). No LinkedIn presence yet — this is real practice with no content engine behind it.

## Segment A — "התקועה/ה בלופ" (Stuck in the Loop) — primary IG audience

**Who:** ages 25–55, in a relationship (married or long-term), Hebrew-speaking, Israel.

**Frustrations** (pulled directly from her own real landing-page copy — this is evidence, not invented persona-building):
- "הריב שחוזר על עצמו" — the same fight regardless of topic
- "לבד בתוך הזוגיות" — feeling alone inside the relationship
- "האינטימיות נשחקה" — eroded intimacy, the relationship became a defense arena
- "ויתור עצמי" — abandoning her own wants just to keep the peace

**Emotional triggers:** fear the relationship "isn't what it used to be"; shame at feeling "too needy" or "too much"; relief and hope when a reframe removes blame ("it's not about who's right").

**Content consumption habits:** scrolls IG evenings (post-bedtime) and weekend mornings; stops on a pain-point hook before an educational one; saves/shares carousels that "explain me to myself" more than tip-lists; engages more with first-person story than abstract advice.

**What converts follow → engage → buy:** content that names the *exact* unspoken feeling accurately (the "how did she know" moment); a visibly lived credibility signal (12 years coaching, not theory); low-pressure entry points (free guide, 5-day challenge — both of which already exist per her background links) before any paid-course ask.

## Segment B — "המנהל/ת בעומס" (The Overloaded Manager) — secondary, mostly untapped

**Who:** team leads/managers, 30–50, under pressure, more likely reached via LinkedIn, referral, or in-person talks than IG.

**Frustrations:** reactive leadership under stress, conflict avoidance or explosion with no middle, team-motivation fatigue, no model for "calm" leadership besides forcing it.

**Emotional triggers:** fear that showing strain reads as weakness or incompetence; relief at hearing "leadership from stability" framed as a learnable skill, not a fixed personality trait.

**Content consumption habits:** long-form/LinkedIn or referral-driven rather than IG carousel-scrolling; responds to demonstrated competence (her own 12 years running an agency) faster than generic leadership-guru content.

**What converts:** practical 3-step frameworks that signal competence quickly; an invitation to a workshop or talk before a personal-coaching pitch.

## Segment C — "בוגרי מחוברים מחדש" (Reconnected graduates) — small, highest-intent

Already trust her; the upsell to the deeper course "חזרה לזוגיות שבחרתי" needs almost no persuasion, just visibility and timely reminders — this is the cheapest segment to convert and should not be neglected just because it's small.

---

## Strategic decision this surfaces — raise as a question tonight, not a verdict

Running both audiences (couples + managers) on one IG feed risks diluting both — neither
reads as "this account is for me." **Recommendation to discuss, not a settled call:** treat
Segment A (couples) as the primary IG identity for now, since that's where her real content
history and existing audience already are. Park Segment B (management) as a parallel
expansion — LinkedIn, talks, a separate content stream — once Segment A is running well,
rather than blending it into the same feed from day one.
