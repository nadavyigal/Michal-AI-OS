## 2. Voice & tone

Two registers depending on audience — both genuinely hers, not a contradiction:

- **Raw / working voice** (seen in her own unedited course notes): direct, second-person,
  body-and-emotion language, repetition for emphasis, lists of symptoms before solutions,
  unpolished and real — typos and all. This is closer to how she actually thinks and talks.
- **Public/brand voice** (seen in her site copy): warm, confident, editorial — short
  declarative sentences, one idea per line, a pull-quote structure, "from the field, not just
  theory" credibility.

She sounds like: *direct, warm, has lived what she teaches, leads with the felt problem before
the framework.*
She never sounds like: *clinical/jargon-heavy, hype-y growth-marketing voice, generic
self-help platitudes.*

## 3. Signature phrases (Hebrew, verbatim from her own writing)

- "מבפנים החוצה" — from the inside out (her core method framing, used for both individuals and orgs)
- "מהשטח, לא רק תיאוריה" / "שנים של שטח" — from the field, not just theory
- "השתקפות" — reflection/mirroring (Satir-method term she uses often)
- "פצעי ילדות" — childhood wounds (recurring in her couples work)

- "ריפוי" — healing (used for both relational and behavioral change)
- "יציבות פנימית" — inner stability
- "תהליך שנשאר" / "שינוי אמיתי שנשאר" — a process/change that actually lasts (her quality bar — explicitly distinguishing real change from a workshop high)

## 4. Sentence & structure

- Short paragraphs, often one sentence per line — built for scanning.
- Hooks open with a felt symptom or question, not a solution ("לפעמים נדמה שהלופ לא נגמר...").
- Numbered/structured breakdowns are core to her teaching style (7-session syllabuses,
  numbered service lists) — she thinks in sequences and frameworks.
- Body copy moves: **symptom → reframe → what's actually possible**. Rarely jumps straight to
  the offer.
- Pull-quotes in first person carry the emotional weight of a section.
- Stats/credibility presented as plain numbers, no overclaiming ("12+ שנות ניהול", "12+
  שנות קואצ'ינג", "20+ עובדים תחת ניהול ישיר").

## 8. Do / Don't (the flat-copy trap)

A prior AI-generated social content draft was found in the source repo
(`old/social_media_content_plan.md`) — it is **generic relationship-coach Instagram voice**:
punchy one-liners, "the soul chose him for a reason," hashtag soup. This is very likely an
example of the **outsourced-copywriter flatness she complained about** — competent but
interchangeable with any coach's account.

| Do | Don't |
|----|-------|
| Open with the specific felt symptom, in her phrasing ("הלופ לא נגמר") | Open with a generic mystical claim ("הנשמה שלך בחרה בו") |
| Use her real method vocabulary (השתקפות, פצעי ילדות, מבפנים החוצה) | Use generic self-help vocabulary anyone could write |
| Ground claims in her own field experience (12 years managing, 12 years coaching) | Make claims that could apply to any coach |
| Numbered, structured frameworks | Vague inspirational lists |
| First-person stories told plainly, undramatized | Manufactured "dramatic moment" stories with tidy dialogue |
